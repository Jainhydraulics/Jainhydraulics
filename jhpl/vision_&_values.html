Vision & Values
JHPL will maintain its position as global leaders in the design and manufacture of high quality, customized equipment for the recycling industries. This will be achieved through continuous investment in research and development of both our products and our people. We will continue to focus on reducing the carbon footprint of our industry through the further development of innovative technologies. JHPL will continue to exceed customer expectations through the provision of innovative, value-added products and a world-class service from specification to installation and beyond.

JHPL will achieve these aims by attracting, training and retaining the most talented and customer focused employees in the industry. We will provide a rewarding workplace environment with a focus on teamwork.
 
The JHPL Values

Products

  • We will design and manufacture the highest quality equipment that the industry can offer. 

  • All of our equipment installations will be designed to suit our customers’ individual requirements.

  • We will continue to add value to our existing product range through a focus on product development and innovation in design.
     
  • We continuously seek to find ways in which we can adapt our existing technologies for use in alternative applications. 

  • Our long established focus on our environmental responsibility will be further developed as we continue to develop ways to help the global industry reduce its carbon footprint. 
Service
  • Our core purpose is to continue to exceed customer expectations. 
     
  • We will provide a world-class service through every stage of every project and beyond, to ensure the delivery of projects, which will out-perform our customers’ expectations.
     
  • We will continue to work in partnership with our customers to ensure their plant continues to operate at maximum efficiency, therefore ensuring maximum profitability. 
 
People
  • We will continue to add the most dynamic, ambitious and talented individuals available to the global JHPL team.
     
  • We will continue to invest in the professional and personal development of all members of the JHPL global team to ensure we continue to offer a superior level of both product and service. 
     
  • We will provide a dynamic, team-focused environment where excellence is rewarded.
