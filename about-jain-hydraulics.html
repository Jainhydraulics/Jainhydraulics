Overview
Since 1973, Jain Hydraulics has been manufacturing the world’s leading recycling equipments. Being a Pioneer, its world class resource pool and advance technology has consistently driven an encouraging change in the Indian Recycling Industry.
 
Today, its vast experience, consistent quality up gradations & continuous R&D has made its products at par with the global standards. Over the years, the company has gathered expertise in providing waste management solutions on turnkey basis with strong environmental commitments.

 Jain Hydraulics is a leading manufacturer of heavy duty Scrap Baling Presses, Shredders & Crushers, Box balers & Shearers, Continuous feed high production Paper Balers, Paper shredders, Slag crushers & finer, Hospital Waste Management Equipments (Bio medical waste sterilizers & Shredders), Solid waste Management Equipments (Solid waste compactors & Shredders), Briquetting Machines, Shearing Machines, Hydraulic Choppers, Bale Breakers, Aluminium Casting Crackers, L.P.G. Cylinder Balers & De-shaping Machines, E.O.T. Cranes, Hydraulic Presses for various applications, Power Packs, Cylinders & Valves.

Jain Hydraulics has marked its excellence not only in India but all around the globe. The Company has major installations in Africa, Australia, Bangladesh, Egypt, France, Georgia, Malaysia, Middle East, Mauritius, Nepal, New Zealand, South Africa, Sri Lanka, Syria, Thailand, West Indies, Zimbabwe.  As an organised manufacturer of these machines, the company has always maintained healthy relations with its customers. Its commitment to work, quality & service has always been a benchmark in the industry for several years because of which BRAND “JH” speaks for itself.

Jain hydraulics is an ISO 9001:2000 certified company and instil globally recognized quality standards and procedures in its manufacturing processes. The company runs Continuous Improvement Programmes in order to develop equipments and systems that create least impact on the environment. Integrity, Engineering Excellence and Customer Delight were always pedestal for its founders and also remain a foresight for the current leaders
