Twin Shaft Shredder

Metal Recycling | Waste Recycling

Designed for efficient size reduction of various materials. It has two parallel shafts with interlocking cutting blades or knives that rotate in opposite directions. The dual-shaft design enhances the shredding capability and reduces the risk of material jams. Twin shaft shredders are versatile can process a wide range of scrap like, including plastics, rubber, metal, paper, and more. The Shredding action break down materials into smaller, more manageable pieces, making them suitable for recycling.
